A Glimpse Into the
Future of Humanity

Daedalus is a comic book that follows the story of two people, Estafos and Suraya, who meet each other by accident and are both trying to find the mystery behind a planet that has trapped them.

They soon discover that they have more in common than they thought and decide to work together to find a way off the planet.

The story is full of action, adventure, and suspense and is sure to keep you entertained from beginning to end. If you're looking for a great new comic book to read, then be sure to check out Daedalus!

Daedalus Game

Daedalus (Drifter) project is a video game that takes you on a thrilling journey on your hoverbike to uncover the secrets of a hostile alien world. You'll explore diverse corners of this mysterious planet and come face-to-face with its dangerous inhabitants.

With stunning graphics and an exciting story, this is one game you won't want to miss out on. You'll never know what's waiting around the next bend or over the next horizon.

With simple yet effective graphics and an intense gameplay, this video game is sure to keep you captivated for hours on end.
